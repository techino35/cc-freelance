Privacy Policy — InvoiceForge

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Overview

InvoiceForge ("the Add-on") is a Google Sheets add-on that generates professional PDF invoices from your spreadsheet data and delivers them via email. This Privacy Policy explains what data is accessed and how it is handled.

2. Data We Collect and Access

Data TypePurpose
Invoice data from your spreadsheet (client name, items, amounts)Populate invoice template and generate PDF
Client email addressesDeliver generated invoices via email
Your business information (name, address, logo)Include on generated invoices; stored in PropertiesService
Invoice template Doc ID and Drive folder settingsStored locally via PropertiesService

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Invoice data is used solely to generate PDF documents from your template.
  • Generated PDFs are saved to your Google Drive and optionally emailed to your clients.
  • The developer does not receive or store your invoice data or client information.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Invoices are delivered only to email addresses you specify.

5. Data Storage

Generated invoice PDFs are stored in your own Google Drive. Configuration and business profile are stored in Google Apps Script's PropertiesService. No data is stored on servers operated by the developer.

6. Google API Services User Data Policy

InvoiceForge's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
