Privacy Policy — MeetBot

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Overview

MeetBot ("the Service") is a meeting transcription and summarization tool that processes audio recordings and generates structured meeting minutes in Google Docs. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is handled.

2. Data We Collect and Access

Data TypePurpose
Audio/video files you uploadExtract audio and transcribe meeting content
Transcription textGenerate structured meeting minutes via AI
Google account credentialsWrite meeting minutes to Google Docs
User email addressAccount identification and job status notifications

3. AI Transcription and Summarization

Audio data is processed using AI transcription services (such as OpenAI Whisper). Transcription text is then processed by an AI language model to generate structured minutes. By using MeetBot, you acknowledge that your audio content will be transmitted to these AI services for processing.

4. Data Retention

  • Uploaded audio files are deleted from our servers immediately after transcription is complete.
  • Transcription text is deleted after meeting minutes are generated and delivered.
  • Generated meeting minutes are stored in your Google Drive only.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties beyond the AI processing services required for transcription and summarization.

6. Security

All data transmission uses HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). Uploaded files are stored temporarily in an isolated processing environment and deleted after job completion.
