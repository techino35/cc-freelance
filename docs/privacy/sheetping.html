Privacy Policy — SheetPing

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Overview

SheetPing ("the Add-on") is a Google Sheets add-on that monitors cell changes and sends notifications to user-configured destinations. This Privacy Policy explains what data is accessed, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Data We Collect and Access

Data TypePurpose
Spreadsheet cell values (selected ranges only)Detect changes in monitored ranges
User email addressInclude editor identity in change notifications
Webhook URLs and email addresses you configureSend notifications to your chosen destinations
Monitoring rules and settingsStored locally via PropertiesService

3. How We Use Your Data

All data is used solely to provide the add-on's notification functionality. Specifically:

  • Spreadsheet data is read only to detect changes — it is never stored on any external server.
  • Notification payloads are sent directly from your Google account to your configured destination (Slack, Discord, email, etc.).
  • The developer does not receive a copy of any notification or spreadsheet data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Notification content is sent only to the destinations you explicitly configure.

5. Data Storage

All settings (webhook URLs, monitoring rules, license keys) are stored exclusively in Google Apps Script's PropertiesService, which is part of your Google account. No data is stored on external servers operated by the developer.

6. Google API Services User Data Policy

SheetPing's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
