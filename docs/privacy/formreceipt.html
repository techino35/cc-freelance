Privacy Policy — FormReceipt

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Overview

FormReceipt ("the Add-on") automatically generates PDF receipts from Google Form responses and emails them to respondents. This Privacy Policy explains what data is accessed and how it is handled.

2. Data We Collect and Access

Data TypePurpose
Google Form responses (all fields)Populate receipt template with respondent data
Respondent email addressSend the generated PDF receipt via email
Google Docs template contentGenerate personalized PDF documents
Template Doc ID and Drive folder IDStored locally via PropertiesService

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Form response data is used solely to populate your configured receipt template.
  • Generated PDFs are saved to your Google Drive folder and emailed to the respondent.
  • The developer does not receive or store any form response data or generated documents.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Generated receipts are delivered only to the email address provided in the form response.

5. Data Storage

Configuration settings (template IDs, folder paths) are stored in Google Apps Script's PropertiesService. Generated PDF files are stored in your own Google Drive. No data is stored on servers operated by the developer.

6. Google API Services User Data Policy

FormReceipt's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
