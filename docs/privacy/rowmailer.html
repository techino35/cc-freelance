Privacy Policy — RowMailer

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Overview

RowMailer ("the Add-on") is a Google Sheets add-on that sends personalized bulk emails to each row in your spreadsheet using a customizable template. This Privacy Policy explains what data is accessed and how it is handled.

2. Data We Collect and Access

Data TypePurpose
Spreadsheet row data (all columns)Populate email template placeholders per recipient
Recipient email addresses (from your sheet)Send personalized emails via Gmail
User email addressSet as the sender / reply-to address
Email template and settingsStored locally via PropertiesService

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Row data is used solely to populate email templates — it is never stored externally.
  • Emails are sent directly from your Gmail account to recipients in your spreadsheet.
  • The developer does not receive copies of emails sent or spreadsheet data.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers other than Gmail's sending infrastructure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Emails are sent only to addresses contained in your spreadsheet.

5. Data Storage

Email templates and configuration are stored in Google Apps Script's PropertiesService. No spreadsheet data is stored externally by the developer.

6. Gmail Access

RowMailer uses Gmail solely to send emails on your behalf. It does not read, modify, or delete any existing emails in your inbox.

7. Google API Services User Data Policy

RowMailer's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
