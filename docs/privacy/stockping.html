Privacy Policy — StockPing

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Overview

StockPing ("the Add-on") is a Google Sheets add-on that monitors inventory levels and sends alerts when stock falls below minimum thresholds. This Privacy Policy explains what data is accessed, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Data We Collect and Access

Data TypePurpose
Spreadsheet cell values (stock data)Detect low-stock conditions in your inventory sheet
User email addressLicense key association
Webhook URLs and email addresses you configureSend stock alert notifications
Column configuration and threshold settingsStored locally via PropertiesService

3. How We Use Your Data

All data is used solely to provide inventory monitoring and alerting functionality:

  • Spreadsheet data is read to check stock levels — it is never stored on any external server.
  • Alert payloads are sent directly from your Google account to your configured destination.
  • The developer does not receive a copy of your inventory data or alerts.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Alert content is sent only to the destinations you explicitly configure.

5. Data Storage

All settings are stored exclusively in Google Apps Script's PropertiesService. No data is stored on external servers operated by the developer.

6. Google API Services User Data Policy

StockPing's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
